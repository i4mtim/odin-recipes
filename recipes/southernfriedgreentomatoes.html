Ingredients

  • 1 extra large egg
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 green tomatoes. sliced

Steps

  1. In a small bowl whisk together egg and milk. In another small bowl mix cornmeal and flour
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat; use more or less oil to reach about 1/4 inch in depth
  3. Dredge tomato slices frist in egg mixture, then in cornmeal mixture. Carefully place slices in hot oil and cook until browned on both sides.
